Women In View

services: Illustration and statistic design
date: January - March 2019
client: Women in View

project overview

Women in View is a national not-for-profit organization dedicated to strengthening gender representation and diversity in Canadian media both on-screen and behind the scenes. They contacted me to create a report for them and help visualize the research that they found through illustrations, infographics, and layout.
