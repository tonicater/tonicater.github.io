about me

I am Toni Cater, a quirky Illustrator and Graphic designer.

I am a graphic designer and illustrator based in Toronto, Canada. I work as a part-time Design and Communications Coordinator at UforChange and a freelance designer. I recently graduated from OCAD University in 2018 with a Bachelors of Design degree and had past experiences teaching graphic design to a variety of different audiences such as Digital Media Academy as an assistant instructor. I am bubbly, patient and always ready to take on a design task. If you like what you see, please drop leave your information in my contact box.
