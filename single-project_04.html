textiles

services: Wall Paper,Textiles, packaging

project overview

Textiles are a simple but affective art form that is dear to my heart. I like to call it my exclusive passion project where I get a chance to beautify and bring simple elegance in unsuspecting homes and spaces.

design process

When I think of how to utilize textiles, I think of how individuals use them to set the premise of existence of creativity in the home. They are a simple way to create statement pieces of wall art without adding to many items on the wall space.
